## Archives and Compression

Used to compress, extract, and manage archived files.

### tar
Creates or extracts tar archives.

Example:
tar -cvf backup.tar /data
tar -xvf backup.tar

---

### gzip
Compresses files.

Example:
gzip file.log

---

### gunzip
Decompresses files.

Example:
gunzip file.log.gz

---

### zip
Creates zip archives.

Example:
zip app.zip app/

---

### unzip
Extracts zip files.

Example:
unzip app.zip

---

### DevOps Usage
- Archiving logs
- Packaging build artifacts
- Backups and data transfer
